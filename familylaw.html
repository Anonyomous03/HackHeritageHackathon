What is Family Law?

Family Law Definition:


.

Family law is a branch of legal practice that deals with matters related to familial relationships and domestic issues. It encompasses a wide range of legal topics, including marriage, divorce, child custody, adoption, spousal support, property division, and issues related to domestic violence. Family law aims to establish and regulate the rights and responsibilities of individuals within a family unit and address disputes that may arise in these areas. It varies from jurisdiction to jurisdiction, as laws and regulations can differ significantly depending on local legal system.
