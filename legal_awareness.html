Q.What are the fundamental rights and how do they
protect citizen in our country ?
A.Fundamental rights in many countries, including India, are a set of basic liberties and privileges guaranteed to citizens by the constitution. In India, these rights include freedoms like the right to equality, freedom of speech and expression, right to life and personal liberty, and more. They protect citizens by ensuring that the government cannot infringe upon these rights arbitrarily, providing a legal framework for citizens to seek redressal if their rights are violated.
Q. How does the legal system handles issues like discrimination,harrassment and human rights violations?
A.Legal remedies may include compensation, injunctions, or criminal penalties depending on the severity of the violation and the specific laws in place. Additionally, many countries have established anti-discrimination and human rights commissions to investigate complaints and promote equal treatment under the law.
